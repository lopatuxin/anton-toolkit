# Idea generation rules

Five rules. Each idea must originate from one of them. Tag the idea with the rule that produced it.

## 1. Gap-fill

Take a gap from `yt-competitors §8`. The video fills the gap directly.

- Strong: niche has 0–2 videos on a topic that has demand evidence (comments asking, outlier video showing demand).
- Weak: a topic nobody covers — could mean no demand. Skip unless paired with demand evidence.

Naming pattern: "<the gap, framed as a video>".

## 2. Series continuation

Take a flagship series (Logos / CRM / similar) and produce the next coherent episode. Use:

- Existing episodes as the pacing anchor (length, format).
- Previous episode's cliffhanger or natural next step.
- A user-channel hypothesis from `yt-my-channel §5` if one points to "more series episodes" winning.

Series episodes carry brand and lock retention to the channel. Aim for 2 series ideas in every batch.

## 3. Format-shift

Take a topic the user already covered well (high view share or strong engagement on `yt-my-channel §2`) and re-cut it in a format/length that the niche favours (`yt-competitors §6`).

Example: user has a 20-min tutorial that did decently; niche top performers in the same topic cluster are 8–12 min — propose a tighter re-cut from a sharper angle.

Do not re-publish the same video. The angle must change.

## 4. Trend-ride

Take a tool or topic that is rising in the niche (recent `getTrendingVideos` data referenced in `yt-competitors`) and apply the user's angle. Two valid angles for this channel:

- Flagship-series treatment (use the trending tool inside Logos/CRM build).
- Vibe-coding workflow (how the tool fits the user's day-to-day).

Avoid pure tool review unless the user has a sharp opinion. Reviews are crowded.

## 5. Lead-gen anchor

Produce a case-study idea designed to convert viewers into clients. Structure:

- A real or representative project class the user can build (CRM, AI assistant, dashboard, automation).
- Story beat: "клиент пришёл с задачей X → собрал за Y дней с помощью Z → результат → вот ссылка для заказа".
- Length tight — 8–14 min. Long-form lead-gen loses warmth.

Aim for 2 lead-gen ideas in every batch. They are why the channel exists commercially.

## Diversity requirements

In a 10–20 idea batch:

- ≥ 2 from rule 2 (series continuation)
- ≥ 2 from rule 5 (lead-gen)
- ≥ 3 from rule 1 (gap-fill)
- Remainder from rules 3 and 4

If the analytical input does not support these minimums (e.g., no gaps reported), say so in the output and adjust — do not invent.

## Drop rules

Drop an idea if:

- No traceable evidence from the input.
- Length estimate > 20 min (channel cap).
- Topic already done by the user in the last 6 months (check `yt-my-channel` recent uploads).
- Idea overlaps with another idea in the batch by > 60% — keep the stronger one.
